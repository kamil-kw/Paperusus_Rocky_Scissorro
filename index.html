Rocky Paperusus Scissorro

The Ultimate Tournament

This are our fighers:

Rocky

Slightly thick-skinned, was born with extraordinary ability to dull sharp tools with his own skin. His eternal enemy is Paperusus – Master of the hammer art.


Paperusus

He may look inconspicuously, but he wields a hammer as no one before. In the far east countries he learned how to combat with the hammer, and he is second to none. With one stroke he can break down the biggest and toughest rocks in the world. His eternal enemy is Scissorro.

Scissorro

A brave hero from the Scissorland, defending the oppressed, trained by his father. Currently the fencing master with a talent to inflicting lethal strokes with unheard of speed. His eternal enemy is Rocky.

WANT TO KNOW HOW TO PLAY?

Game RULES?

VS
Players

You as a player are going to fight against the machine(computer)


Rounds

The tournament have 10 rounds, however (!important) the number of rounds depends on the end scoring (draw is not an option!). The game will end only when there is a winner. By default that should happen within 10 rounds, but this number is not “solid” and might change as explained.


Throw Strategy

As all in this world also our heros have a cons and pros, see details below:

Rocky - due to his extraordinary ability to dull sharp tools with his own skin he will destroy the Scissioro, however his nemesis is Paperusus

Paparusus - As he can crush with one stroke the biggest and toughest rocks in the world he will crush the Rocky into the piecies, however his nemesis is Scissoro

Scissioro - As the fencing master with a talent to inflicting lethal strokes with unheard of speed he will cut in piecies the Paparusus, however his nemesis is Rocky


You may ask how can I play this game

Is easy as mouse clicking, just choose your character out of three characters from top side of the screen and then immediately the machine will choose (random) its character to fight against you
